About Us

The mission of Tech -M Technologies is to provide IT services for Kenyan citizen and great job opportunities to African youth by training them on digital, software and other technologies that prepare them for the future of work by equipping them with marketable, industry driven skills through industrial attachment. Our goal is to ensure that our talented youth grow in order to meet the growing opportunities and needs of the ICT Industry in Kenya. We also envision an explosion of technology innovation and the creation of successful tech ventures by local developers from across the country with a zeal for changing the world through harnessing the power of their technology training for social good.
